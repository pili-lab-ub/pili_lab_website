Our Research Projects

The Pili Lab conducts translational oncology research, focusing on genitourinary maligancies. This includes the following areas of active research:

  • Developing histone deacetylase inhibitors in genitourinary cancers
  • Developing rational combination strategies with TKIs
  • Overcoming drug resistance mechanisms
  • Clinical evaluation of agents targeting the tumor microenvironment
  • Evaluation of dietary modifications on tumor microenvironment and immune function
