Overcoming Drug Resistance Mechanisms

A major focus of our lab has been to identify molecular mechanisms responsible for drug resistance. Our group has been among the first to characterize the resistance to anti-VEGF therapies in renal cell carcinoma as a transient phenomenon likely due to epithelial-to-mesenchymal transition and driven by epigenetic changes. More recently, we have reported the role of YAP in cisplatin resistance in patient-derived xenograft models of bladder cancer.
