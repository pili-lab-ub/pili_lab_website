Clinical Evaluation of Agents Targeting the Tumor Microenvironment

Our group has been involved in the clinical testing of several agents targeting the tumor microenvironment, including antiangiogenics and immunomodulators. These studies have generated the preliminary data for further development of these therapeutic strategies.
