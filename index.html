Welcome to Our Lab

The Pili laboratory is a translational oncology lab, focused on the role of epigenetic modifications in overcoming drug resistance and modulating response to immunotherapies. The laboratory is also studying the impact of dietary interventions as adjuvant tools in patients receiving cancer treatments.
